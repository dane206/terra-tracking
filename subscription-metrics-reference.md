# Subscription Executive Metrics Reference

## 1. Net Revenue Retention (NRR)

**Definition:**
Measures how subscription revenue from existing customers changes over time, including expansions, downgrades, and churn.

**Formula:**

```
NRR = (Starting Revenue + Expansion Revenue − Churned Revenue − Downgraded Revenue) 
      ÷ Starting Revenue
```

**Interpretation:**

* < 100% → Revenue is shrinking
* 100% → Revenue is flat
* 110%+ → Strong retention and expansion
* 120%+ → Elite performance

---

## 2. Churn Rate

### a) Customer Churn Rate

**Definition:**
Percentage of subscribers lost during a period.

**Formula:**

```
Customer Churn = Lost Subscribers ÷ Total Subscribers at Start of Period
```

---

### b) Revenue Churn Rate

**Definition:**
Percentage of recurring revenue lost during a period.

**Formula:**

```
Revenue Churn = Lost Recurring Revenue ÷ Total Recurring Revenue at Start
```

**Note:** Revenue churn is often more important than customer churn.

---

## 3. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Definition:**
Total expected contribution profit from a customer over their lifetime.

**Basic Formula (Subscription Model):**

```
LTV = Average Monthly Contribution Margin ÷ Monthly Churn Rate
```

Alternative simplified version:

```
LTV = Average Revenue per User (ARPU) × Average Customer Lifetime
```

---

## 4. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Definition:**
Average cost to acquire one paying subscriber.

**Formula:**

```
CAC = Total Marketing & Sales Spend ÷ Number of New Customers Acquired
```

---

## 5. LTV : CAC Ratio

**Definition:**
Measures long-term customer value relative to acquisition cost.

**Formula:**

```
LTV : CAC = Lifetime Value ÷ Customer Acquisition Cost
```

**Benchmarks:**

* < 2:1 → Unsustainable
* 3:1 → Healthy
* 4:1+ → Scalable
* 5:1+ → Aggressive growth opportunity

---

## 6. Contribution Margin

**Definition:**
Profit remaining after all variable costs are deducted.

**Formula:**

```
Contribution Margin = Revenue 
                      − Cost of Goods Sold 
                      − Shipping 
                      − Payment Processing Fees 
                      − Variable Support Costs
```

---

## 7. Contribution Margin Payback Period

**Definition:**
Time required to recover CAC from contribution profit.

**Formula:**

```
Payback Period (Months) = CAC ÷ Monthly Contribution Margin per Subscriber
```

**Benchmarks:**

* < 3 months → Extremely strong
* 3–6 months → Scalable
* 6–12 months → Capital intensive
* 12+ months → High risk unless funded

---

# Executive Summary View

The three most critical subscription metrics for scaling:

1. **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)** → Are customers compounding revenue?
2. **Churn Rate** → How fast are we leaking value?
3. **Payback Period** → How fast does acquisition cash return?
